About Us

The surf club aims to connect fellow surfers to improve their technique and have fun. We are open to any current Punahou academy students who are active wave riders.

Club meetings are held monthly over Webex Meetings to discuss surfing technique, the wave forecast, fun stories, etc.

We will compete in HSA high school surf contests as soon as they are up and running again.
